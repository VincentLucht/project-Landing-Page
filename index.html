My journey of programming
When I was really young, I had access to a phone and I was instantly hooked to the Internet. I always wondered how it worked and how to make a website, but I always and thought: "It's to complicated for me anyway" and pushed it away.

After graduating from high school, I wondered what I should do now, and then bam: I saw a video about a programming course called "CS50x" and that it is from Harvard and completely free? I was baffled, and immediately signed up and started it. It was a grueling process, but I managed my way trough it.

It was difficult, but I only learned the fundamentals from it, and I jumped to another course from Dr. Angela Yu, where I became very proficient with Python. But I also wanted to learn web development, so that I could display all my projects on cool websites, but Python couldn't do that and them bam! - I found out about the incredible (and free) "The Odin Project", which is what I am currently doing!
